# LibreBase Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 3 June 2026_

LibreBase is built so that your data never leaves your device. It has no
accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

## What LibreBase does

- **Bluetooth** is used solely to find your QardioBase scale and read each
  weigh-in. LibreBase does not connect to any other device and sends nothing
  over the network.
- **Apple Health** — with your permission, LibreBase writes your weight (and the
  height you enter) to Apple Health, and reads your height to calculate BMI in
  the app. This data is stored by Apple Health on your device, under your
  control. You can revoke access at any time in Settings ▸ Privacy & Security ▸
  Health.

## Data we collect

**None.** LibreBase does not collect, transmit, store on any server, sell, or
share any personal data. There are no third-party SDKs, no advertising, and no
usage analytics.

## Children's privacy

LibreBase does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Open source

LibreBase is open source. You can review exactly what it does at
<https://github.com/stormychel/LibreBase>.

## Medical disclaimer

LibreBase is a wellness logging tool, not a medical device.
